Comprehensive Repair Services

  • Jammed Disposal Units : A common issue with garbage disposals is jamming, often due to improper use or attempting to grind non-compatible items. Our technicians can swiftly identify and resolve blockage issues, restoring your system to full functionality.

  • Leaking Garbage Disposals : Leaks can cause significant water damage if not addressed promptly. Our repair services include a thorough examination to pinpoint the source of leaks, whether due to faulty seals or worn-out rings, ensuring comprehensive solutions.

  • Unusual Noises : Grinding, rattling, or humming sounds are indicators of mechanical problems. We specialize in diagnosing and repairing these issues, whether they stem from loose parts or foreign objects lodged within the unit.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common signs include unusual noises, frequent resets, poor performance, leaks, and unpleasant odors. If your unit is consistently jamming or clogging, it might also be a sign of underlying issues.

Garbage disposals generally last between 8 to 15 years, depending on usage and maintenance. If your unit is vibrating excessively, making odd noises, or not disposing waste effectively, it may be time for a replacement.

While it is possible to install a garbage disposal yourself if you have plumbing and electrical skills, hiring a professional ensures safety and proper installation, minimizing potential problems later on.

First, turn off the disposal and unplug it for safety. Use a flashlight to check for visible obstructions and remove them with tongs or pliers. Avoid using your hands. If the issue persists, consider using a disposal wrench or contacting a professional.

Regular maintenance includes running cold water during and after use, avoiding non-degradable waste like fats or fibrous foods, and occasionally cleaning the unit with a mixture of baking soda and vinegar to neutralize odors and prevent build-up.
